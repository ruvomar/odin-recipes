Chicken Kabob With Zesty Garlic Marinade

This is a very easy and yet delicious recipe for amazing chicken kabobs! Note if you use bamboo skewers, have them soaked in water for 1 hour to prevent sticking.

Recipe was taken from The Mediterranean Dish and all credit goes to author for this delicious recipe. Please check out the rest of their site for other great recipes!

  • Ingredients for Marinade

    • Lemon/lime juice
    • Extra virgin olive oil
    • Garlic or garlic powder
    • Spanish paprika - smoked or sweet
    • Ground nutmeg
    • Ground cardamom
    • Dried thyme
    • Salt and pepper

  • Primary Ingredients

    • Chicken - boneless, skinless breast
    • Yellow onion
    • Bell peppers - green and red
    • Sweet red onions

  • Directions

    • Making the spice blend: In a small bowl, add 2 teaspoons paprika, 1 teaspoon dried thyme, 1 teaspoon ground nutmeg, ¼ teaspoon ground green cardamom. Add a generous pinch of salt and pepper and mix to combine.
    • Prepping the chicken: Cut 2 pounds of boneless, skinless chicken breasts into 1 ½-inch cubes. Set in a large bowl and pour the spice mix on top. Use your hands to generously coat the chicken with the spices.
    • Marinate the chicken: In a small bowl, whisk together 15 minced garlic cloves and the juice of 3 lemons (or 6 limes). Add 1 sliced yellow onion to the bowl with the chicken, then pour the lemon juice mixture over top. Add a generous drizzle of extra virgin olive oil. Toss the chicken to ensure it's well-coated. Cover and refrigerate for 2 to 4 hours for the best results. This is a great time to get your fixings ready and your bamboo skewers soaking.
    • Skewer: Slice a red onion, red bell pepper, and green bell pepper into 1 ½-inch pieces. A few minutes before grilling, thread the chicken, bell peppers, and red onion onto the skewers (discard the yellow onion, it was just to flavor the chicken). Lightly oil and heat a gas grill or griddle to medium-high heat.
    • Grill: Grill the chicken kabobs, turning occasionally until chicken is cooked through (internal temperature should register 165°F), 10-12 minutes. Now serve and enjoy this fabulous dish!
