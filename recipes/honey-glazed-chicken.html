Honey Glazed Chicken

This is a very easy and yet delicious recipe for some delicious chicken! This recipe is so simple you can even add other spices and garnishes to change the flavor, including serving this over a bed of lettuce, rice or anything else you can think of!

Recipe was taken from All Recipes and all credit goes to author for this delicious dish. Please check out the rest of their site for other great recipes!

  • Ingredients

    • 1/4 cup of honey
    • 2 tablespoons of soy sauce
    • 1/8 teaspoon of red pepper flakes
    • 1 1/2 tablespoons of olive oil
    • 2 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves, cut into bite-size pieces

  • Directions

    1. Whisk honey, soy sauce, and red pepper flakes in a bowl; set aside.
    2. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat, cook and stir chicken in hot oil until lightly brown, about 5 minutes.
    3. Pour honey mixture into the skillet; continue to cook and stir until chicken is no longer pink in the center and sauce is thickened, about 5 minutes more.
    4. Serve hot and enjoy!
